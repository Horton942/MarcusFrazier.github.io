Examine and assess a variety of applications within the field of robotics.

Assignment 1: M5Core2 IMU Sensors

In this assignment, we learned how to code the Inertial Measurement Unit inside of our M5Cores's to get them to take measurements and do things when conditions are met. The core2's IMU senser includes accelerometers, gyroscopesm and magnerometers. in this assignment, we set the devices to record the max variables that the sensors have read and coded a button to reset the values. Then we had the screen blink red if the accelerometer senses a drop of 10 cm or more.

Assignment 2: Final AVR Project

This was my final project for RBT 211 Embedded programing. I combined the potentiometer
to control the LED Light up meter that also controls the servo motor connected to a fan.
When I turn the potentiometer, the fan will either speed up or slow down and the LEDs connected
will reflect the power output. The more LEDs lit, the faster the motor is going. This can be applied to
almost any microcontroller project to give a physical indicater of how much power is being supplied to a
system and the potentiometer makes it controlable.
