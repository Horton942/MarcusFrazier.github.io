Welcome to my Student Innovation Project: Scoot Alert

Description

Scoot alert is an automatic horn system for alerting cars of the presences of a scooter or biker. The horn is activated by a servo motor that is controlled by a
Raspberry pi 5 with an AI hat +2, a raspberry pi AI camera, and an ultrasonic sensor. When the AI camera detects a car with 90% or more confidence within the
detection box and is within 4 feet detected by the ultrasonic sensor, the motor will turn, activating the horn making a blast of sound until the car is no longer
detected for another 5 consecutive frames.
