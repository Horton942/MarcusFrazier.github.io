Welcome to my Student Innovation Project: Scoot Alert

Scoot alert is an automatic horn system for alerting cars of the presences of a scooter or biker. The horn is activated by a servo motor that is controlled by a Raspberry pi 5 with an AI hat +2, a raspberry pi AI camera, and an ultrasonic sensor. With the AI camera detects a car with 90% or more confidence within the detection box and is within 4 feet detected by the ultrasonic sensor, the motor will turn, activating the horn making a blast of sound until the car is no longer detected.
