Robotics Engineering Objective 3

Develop mechanical control systems by implementing transducers, actuators, feedback, vision and sensing systems, and other mechanical systems into robotic platforms.

Assignment 1: M5Core2 IMU Sensors

In this assignment, we learned how to code the Inertial Measurement Unit inside of our M5Cores's to get them to take measurements and do things when conditions are met. The core2's IMU senser includes accelerometers, gyroscopesm and magnerometers. Ie screen blink red if the accelerometer senses a drop of 10 cm or more.

Assignment 2: Building an Application

This assignment was my final for Internet of things where we needed to utilize our M5Core2's to make an application. For my application, I wrote a script that would read the GPS location of the GPS module from the M5Core2 and store it online. The location Latitude and Longitude gets sent online to Thingspeak for storage every 20 seconds. In addition, the current Latitude and Longitude are displayed on the M5Core2 screen.
