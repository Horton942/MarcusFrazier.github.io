Model, analyze, and design systems or processes that integrate hardware and software to control autonomous mechanical systems.

Assignment 1: Servo Control

In this assignment, we utilized our arduino unos to control the movements of a servo motor. utilizing the
L293D motor driver chips externally, we coded our motors to go through a full 180 degree motion forward and back.
After that, We added code to make them jump to start mid and end point or 0 degrees, 90 degrees and 180 degrees. The system then
loops to do it all over again until I disconect from power.

Assignment 2: DC Motor Movement

In this assignment, we simulated moving a DC motor. Here in Part B of the assignment, we were tasked with coding the motor on the Arduino to
move with an ultrasonic sensor. It is coded so that the motor spins at full speed or 255 bits at the sensors max distance. At the
closest distance the motor would spin at it's lowest speed. In the code I set it so that anything below 5 centimeters, the motor stops
completely.
