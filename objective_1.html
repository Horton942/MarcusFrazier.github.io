Assignment 1: final AVR Project

This was my final project for RBT 211 Embedded programing. I combined the potentiometer
to control the LED Light up meter that also controls the servo motor connected to a fan.
When I turn the potentiometer, the fan will either speed up or slow down and the LEDs connected
will reflect the power output. The more LEDs lit, the faster the motor is going. This can be applied to
almost any microcontroller project to give a physical indicater of how much power is being supplied to a
system and the potentiometer makes it controlable.
