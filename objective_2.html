Assignment 1: Motor Speed Controller

For this assignment, We coded our arduinos to run a motor at certain intervals at different speeds Through PWM (Pulse Width Modulation).
It will run the motor at 50% power, then 75%, then 25% which will make it stop because there is not enough power being supplied to the motor,
and finally 100% power and will continue looping until I stop it. In the code, the speeds are mesured in bits from 0-255. 128 bits for 50%, 191 bits for 75%,
64 bits for 25%, and 255 for 100% power.

Assignment 2: Bit Visualizer

In this asignment, we coded our arduinos to control a set of 8 LEDs connect together to work with a potentiometer and then again
with a Photoresister. with the potentiometer, the more turned up the more LEDs light up and with the Photoresister, the more light the
the higher the resistance, and the less LEDs light up. Everything was controlled on our Arduino uno 3s. We coded the onboard
L293D chips to control all 8 LEDs.
